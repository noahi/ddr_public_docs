➕ Create Your Account#

In order to access DataDistillr (DDR) go to app.datadistllr.io and click on sign up. Enter your name, email, and password and then click the Create Account button. An account verification message will be sent to the provided email address and you may also wish to check "Junk/Spam" folder(s) if not present in your Inbox. Once the verification has been activated, you will then be able to access your DataDistillr account.

Once you have created your account and validated your email, nagivate to the log in screen as shown below. If you forgot your password, there is a link to reset your password.
